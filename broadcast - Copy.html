Australia - Country Pavilion at the Gartner Symposium
Ten member organizations were showcased at the Country Pavilion. 1600+ visitors were CIOs, CTOs and Decision makers
on technology partnerships with high representation from BFSI, Retail, Healthcare, Education, Government, Real Estate & Utilities. Additional benefits like CIOs Roundtable, Analyst interactions, Exhibition Space and Lead Generation were designed. NASSCOM will participate in 2019 edition.
Japan - NASSCOM IT Corridor
1st IT Corridor with Hiroshima Prefecture was announced in Oct 2018, with USD 3 Million funding from Hiroshima Government. The corridor will be launched in 2019.
NASSCOM organized IT corridor pre-launch event with Hiroshima in March 2019 with the objective of identifying early adopters of the platform from both sides. The event had focused B2B sessions between leading Hiroshima-based companies and Indian IT companies. It saw participation from 70+ local companies and 18+ Indian companies. During the B2B sessions, 3 leading Hiroshima-based companies shared their needs and showed willingness to partner with Indian companies.
